Sound control technology

Sound control technology
(Noise filtering + Voice transmission)

ALLTALK’s patented noise filtering cancels noise and amplifies only the human voice. No difficult of removing earplugs to talk to others like conventional products.
With ALLTALK, it is not difficult to talk with other people.
It is safe to have easy communication without being disturbed by noise.

Sound screen technology

There is risk of hearing loss if increase the earphone volume due to external noise during online lectures and music listening. ALLTALK’s sound screen technology minimizes external noise so can enjoy clear sound at small volume event outdoors, Preventing hearing loss.
